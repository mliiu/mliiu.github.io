Climate Change Interactive





Previous Next


Global temperatures are on the rise and 70% of man-made pollution is caused by 10 companies. Rather than making individuals on the consumer level responsible, it is up to large oil companies to cut back on emissions.

My group partner, Bei, and I created an immersive visual website (utilizing HTML/CSS, Javascript) that imagines a bleak world where the consequence of climate change is irreversible. We decided to visualize data from the Carbon Majors Report (2017), to create a compelling point-of-view in the form of storytelling.
