Which A24 Film Are You by Location?





I enjoy watching A24 films because all of their movies are able to perfectly encapsulate the feeling of a location in its storytelling. I've created an interactive map documenting where A24 films are popular in the U.S. based on Google Trend searches and demographics during the films' domestic release dates. Sidenote: As someone who grew up and went to school where Lady Bird (2017) was mainly filmed (not as much in Sacramento as you would be tricked to believe), I am a bit salty it did not claim LA.
